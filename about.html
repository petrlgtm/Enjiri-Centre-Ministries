Our Story

A Legacy of Faith & Service

Enjiri Center Ministries International was founded on a deep conviction to spread the gospel of Jesus Christ and serve communities with the love of God. From humble beginnings, the ministry has grown into a vibrant community of believers.

Through the years, God has been faithful in expanding our reach, touching lives across cities and nations. Our doors are open to everyone — regardless of background or walk of life.

President & Founder

Evangelist Peter Kalagi

President, Enjiri Center Ministries

God called Evangelist Kalagi Peter into the ministry of evangelism at the age of 14. Starting from serving in choir, PA systems, youth, and outreach evangelism, he grew through prayer, fasting, and studying the Word of God. After leadership training at Miracle Bible College, his passion for winning souls continued to grow. He has travelled extensively preaching and teaching, leading many souls to Christ both locally and internationally — with bodies healed supernaturally and hearts set free by the power of Almighty God.
